About Us

At Turba, Inc. we want to create an environment in which having a sexual encounter is safer than it is today. We offer people the possibility to choose their next sexual encounter not only for their qualities but for the sexual health of the individual. We are creating an information bank where people can access sexual health reports of other individuals, and thus reduce the risks of infection which, according to the CDC, 30% of annual HIV infections are transmitted by those who do not know they are carriers.

Our goals are focused on reducing infections in addition to helping those who are already infected, with proper treatment some diseases can be cured, and therefore the individual can return to having a healthy sex life. Others who have incurable diseases, can also benefit from treatments that erase infection threats. The only way to make a good decision is by being informed. With your support we will create a safer world for everyone.

Our App: Sexual Reports (SR)

We are a bank of data, where members share STD and HIV result with others. It’s free as should be! The key for entering our data bank is to share yours! To become a member you only have to share your test results with us and that’s it, you have access to our data! To share you only have to go to your favorite clinical laboratory take an STD test and HIV test, then you authorize the laboratory to send the result to us, we upload the results and send a confirmation that allows you to make a free account with us to have access to our data around the world.
